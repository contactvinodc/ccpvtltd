CC
MILESTONES
01

Starting its base as K S Engineering Company registered in 1990as a proprietary concern with 20 manpower servicing simple structural fabrication and erection works carried out in HINDALCO Industries ltd. With gradual increase in manpower we have achieved trust by setting an example of integrated relationship with the 1st company by still pursuing it.

02

Next stage of our development was movement out of Renukoot. “Hindustan Dorr Oliver” was the major client with whom we moved to various sites. Serving in- directly to various clients like Balco, Nalco, UCIL, Vedanta, Utkal, Malco etc.

03

Third stage of development evolved with directly serving the parent company. With the satisfaction of the services provided we were rewarded with the operation, maintenance and manpower supply jobs at Hindalco, Balco, Nalco, Vedanta etc. We also had the opportunity to serve companies like Macawbar India, TPS infra, Stewards and Lloyds India etc. during this stage of growth.

04

Forth stage of development was being awarded by operation and maintenance (O&M) job at Vedanta Aluminium for red mud filter at Vedanta Aluminium Lanjigarh and O&M of red mud filter at Utkal Aluminium Int. Limited till successful handover.

05

In next phase of our growth we carried instrumentation project at Gujarat Credo Alumina Chemicals PVT. LTD. involving erection, testing, calibration & commissioning of Instrumentation System for green field project.

06

Next gem embedded in our profile was being awarded with a LSTK package which included civil mechanical, instrumentation and electrical job at Vedanta Aluminium Lanjigarh.

07

We have secured classification of “A grade” in vendor list of Vedanta Lanjigarh. Also achieved appreciation as cash price for best contractor award 2011 in HINDALCO Industries Limited.

08

  • ISO 9001:2015 for quality management system.
  • OHSAS 18001:2007 for occupational health and safety management systems.
  • MSME for Medium Enterprise
